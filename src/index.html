Fatou B Ceesay (FOUNDER & CEO)

From humble beginnings in The Gambia, Fatou B Ceesay has defied the odds to become a successful and inspiring entrepreneur. With a strong business acumen and creative vision, she has established a range of ventures, including a salon, cleaning business, and doll line.

Through her dedication to empowerment and representation, particularly for black children, Fatou has left an indelible mark on the global business stage. Her story of resilience and triumph is a testament to the power of determination and the limitless possibilities of entrepreneurship.
